1. **Vegan Buddha Bowl:** Ingredients: - 1 cup cooked quinoa or rice - 1 cup chickpeas (cooked or canned) - 1 cup roasted or steamed vegetables (e.g., broccoli, sweet potatoes, carrots) - 1/2 avocado, sliced - 1/4 cup hummus - 2 tbsp tahini sauce (tahini mixed with lemon juice, water, and garlic) - 1 tbsp sesame seeds (optional) - Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: 1. Arrange the cooked quinoa or rice in a bowl as the base. 2. Add the chickpeas, roasted vegetables, and sliced avocado on top. 3. Drizzle the hummus and tahini sauce over the bowl. 4. Sprinkle with sesame seeds, salt, and pepper. 5. Mix everything together and enjoy!
