2. **Vegan Stir-Fry:** Ingredients: - 1 cup firm tofu, cubed - 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, carrots, etc.) - 3 tbsp soy sauce or tamari - 2 tbsp vegetable oil - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1-inch ginger, grated - 1 tbsp cornstarch - 1/4 cup vegetable broth or water - Cooked rice or noodles for serving Instructions: 1. In a bowl, mix the soy sauce, garlic, ginger, cornstarch, and vegetable broth. 2. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat. 3. Add the cubed tofu and stir-fry until lightly browned on all sides. 4. Add the mixed vegetables and continue stir-frying until they are cooked but still crisp. 5. Pour the sauce mixture over the tofu and vegetables, stirring to coat everything evenly. 6. Cook for another minute or until the sauce thickens. 7. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles.
